System Requirements

All you need is a computer and an internet connection.

Computers need not be high end gaming machines. A simple laptop would do. Although Patient Database runs well on mobile devices, it is recommended that you use something with a keyboard to hasten the process of data entry and data retrieval.

Patient Database runs on Windows, iOs, and Linux machines.


Typical Set Up

A typical clinic set up would involve a laptop for your secretary and a laptop for you. Both of you could use the system simultaneously. Your secretary can receive patients, create appointments, and initially create and populate patient visits (height, weight, blood pressure, etc.). When the patient comes in, you (the doctor) can then open the patient's visit page and complete the process. It is that simple.


How do I start?

Everything starts with registration. Simply register HERE and a confirmatory email will be sent to you. Click on the email link and the PxDBase website will be opened to process your request. Upon confirmation, you may start using the system.

Please make sure that you check your email's junk folder because sometimes, emails do wind up in there.


Secretaries

Patient Database classifies all other users under you as secretaries. They can be nurses, encoders, or, as the name implies, secretaries.

Adding secretaries to your account also starts by registering their basic information in the secretaries page. A confirmatory email will be sent to them which contains a link to the confirmation page.

Allow your secretaries to click that confirmatory link in order to let Patient Database to process their account. Only then will they be able to use the system.
