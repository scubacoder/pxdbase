About Us

We are a startup company from the Philippines made up of a team of brilliant web developers with nearly decades of experience under our belt.

This project was the brainchild of our CEO and was aimed originally for the use of his wife's medical practice. Due to the requests of other medical practitioners, it was decided that this should be made available to every doctor in need of a great Clinic Management System.

After a few policies regarding security and privacy were implemented, it is now available FREE of charge!


Is the system complete?

Although all the basic functionalities for a great Clinic Management System are available in Patient Database, our team will never consider this system as complete. It will continue to grow. Aided by suggestions from its medical users, features will gradually be installed and be made available to everyone.

We hope that everyone will find our system useful. Enjoy!
